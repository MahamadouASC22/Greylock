Our approach

Your data. Your rules.

Somewhere along the way, your information stopped belonging to you. Everything we do is built on handing the controls back — with a advisor beside you, and every step in plain English.

No more messes.

Our advisors help you clean it all up and then help you stay organized. Whether it's a vault, password manager, or even figuring out where to get started. Our advisors will help you learn and understand everything you need to keep you protected.

Understanding is security.

A setting you don’t understand is a setting you don’t control.Your advisor will explain every change as you make it - because the goal is to identify the choices that are really important and make them feel like everyday knowledge.

Your apps serve you.

Your joyful and convenient online life doesn’t have to spread your personal data all over the web. Your Privacy Advisor helps you choose what information to share, curate the services you already use, and selectively add the security services that we recommend. We measure our work by how much of your digital life is back in your hands.

You should grow stronger, not more dependent.

Every quarter with should leave you more fluent - spotting the scam, applying the most powerful tools to the accounts that matter the most, navigating the web with the ease and confidence that comes from knowing what’s important. You should leave every conversation knowing more, not owing more.

You leave educated and with value.

From the initial onboarding session, you're learning. If it's spotting the fake bank call or hearing why a credit freeze beats monitoring, at the end of the day, your leaving with value.

The research is our job.

Password managers, removal services, credit monitors, authenticators — a maze of look-alike products and five-star reviews written by nobody. We navigate it for a living, so you never have to.

We test the tools

Before anything earns a place in your life, it earns a place in ours. We run the services ourselves, read the security audits, and retire a recommendation the moment something safer appears.

We read the fine print

Privacy policies, data-sharing clauses, auto-renewal traps — we read the pages nobody reads and tell you what they actually say, before you sign anything.

We match, we never sell

No commissions, no inventory, no side deals. When we recommend a tool, it's because it fits you — your habits, your devices, your family — and for no other reason.
