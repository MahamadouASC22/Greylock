Our approach

Your data has been making decisions without you.

Every account you open, every app you install, every box you check quietly decides who knows what about you. We exist to hand those decisions back — researched, explained, and finally yours to make.

Where we stand

Control was never supposed to require a second job.

Somewhere along the way, "protecting your data" became homework. Forty-page privacy policies. Settings buried five menus deep. Review sites paid by the products they review. The honest answer to "am I safe?" became "it depends — how many weekends do you have?"

We think that's backwards. You shouldn't have to become an expert in an industry that profits from your confusion. So we became the expert instead — an independent advisor with no products to sell you, whose only job is figuring out what's actually best for your life and putting it in plain English.

We do the research. We bring you the answer. You make the call. Every time.

A month with Greylock Trust

What you see — and what's working behind it.

Being in control shouldn't mean doing everything yourself. Here's a real picture of a typical month: your side of the ledger, and ours.

What you experience

Week one

A call from your advisor: a shopping site you use changed how it shares purchase data. Two-minute explanation, one clear recommendation. You say yes, and it's handled while you're on the phone.

Week two

Week three

Monitoring breach disclosures against your accounts, vetting an update to your password manager, and re-checking that your credit freezes are still holding.

Reviewing everything we found this month and translating it into that one readable page — because clarity is the deliverable, not the fine print.

Our promises

Four commitments, in writing

These aren't values on a poster. They're the working rules for every session, every recommendation, and every report — and you should hold us to them.

Promise oneYou hold the keys

Your passwords are typed by your hands. Your changes are approved by your voice. We never store your credentials, never act without your sign-off, and never sell or share a byte of your information. Control isn't a feature we offer — it's the entire point of hiring us.

Promise twoThe research is done for you

Before any recommendation reaches you, we've read the reviews and the fine print, tested the settings, and compared the alternatives — independently. We take no commissions and carry no favorites, so the answer we bring is simply what's best for you.

Promise threePlain English, or it isn't finished

If we can't explain a recommendation clearly enough that you could explain it to someone else, we go back and do more work. Every "what" comes with a "why." Every question is a good question. Jargon is our failure, never yours.

Promise fourA person, not a product

You get one advisor who knows your setup, your family, and your comfort level — not a chatbot, a ticket number, or a dashboard. When something feels off at 9pm on a Sunday, you know exactly who to call, and they know exactly who you are.
