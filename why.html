Why us

Security is a service, not a subscription.

Antivirus software sends you alerts. We send you a person. Here's what makes Greylock Trust different from every app, plan, and product that promises to keep you safe.

Our story

Named for a mountain. Built like one.

Greylock Trust takes its name from Mount Greylock — a summit people have navigated by for centuries. That's the role we play in our clients' digital lives: a fixed point you can rely on, whatever the weather.

We started with a simple observation: the people most targeted by scams, fraud, and identity theft are the least served by the security industry. Corporations get dedicated teams. Everyone else gets an app, a helpline, and a manual written for engineers.

So we became the bridge. Real cybersecurity professionals, working one-on-one with real people — setting things up properly, explaining them patiently, and staying on watch year-round.

"Our job is simple: you should never have to become a technology expert to be safe."
