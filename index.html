What we do

Three steps. Zero homework.

You don't need to learn cybersecurity. You just need someone in your corner who already knows it.

Step 01

Your onboarding session

During a personalized one-on-one meeting, your advisor recommends trusted protections we've already vetted, helps configure your accounts, and explains every recommendation in plain English so you understand what's protecting you and why.

◆ Up to three hours, entirely at your pace
Step 02

We stay on watch

Your protection keeps working in the background. If something looks off — a suspicious login, a data breach, a scam targeting people like you — your advisor reaches out before it becomes a problem.

◆ A real person, watching out for you
Step 03

Quarterly check-ins & reports

Every three months, your advisor reviews your digital security, updates protections as needed, and provides an easy-to-understand report so you always have a clear picture of your digital security and can make informed decisions with confidence.

◆ Written for humans, not engineers
